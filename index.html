House of Elders

Family first

House of Elders is a community of adult gamers, mostly parents, who play Xbox. Our main focus is to make the most of the limited game time we have by playing with liked minded friends. We originate from the Destiny community and are now expanding. We play predominantly in the UK or similar time zones. It is adults only, a place away from the little ones where you can kick back and have fun. Have a look around, check out the forum, jump in and enjoy. HairyDee Founder
